Meet Neelam


Story: Jovie is another victim of a hit and run. The accident fractured her hind limb. We first tried to save her leg with an orthopedic surgery. Unfortunately, it was not successful, so we needed to amputate. After her recovery, we decided not to release her back on the streets as she had already gone through so much.

Personality: This sweet girl is a bit shy. It can take time for her to open up to new people, but once she does, she is fully yours! What she most adores is getting cuddles and kisses from the staff. When she is not getting human attention she also likes to wrestle with her doggie friends and take naps in the sun.







This beautiful tripod puppy is now healthy and pretty much just wants a loving family to live with forever. At the shelter she likes to play with canine companions in the yard, cuddling with the team and taking naps in the sun. She will be a perfect companion for outdoor activities and television sessions with his human. Who will be lucky enough to adopt this gorgeous girl? Contact us today!
